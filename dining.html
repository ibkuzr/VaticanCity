Did all that exploring make you hungry?

Arlù

A restaurant in St. Peter's, Arlù is a pleasant and familiar atmosphere where you can enjoy lunch and dinner made with natural ingredients, with an excellent balance between quality and price. For over fifty years Arlù has been welcoming tourists and lovers of good food in an unpretentious atmosphere, furnished with style and elegance.

http://www.ristorantearlu.it/en/
La Zanzara

La Zanzara, the new Italian bistrot with an international feel in the heart of the Prati district. From Breakfast to After the theater, including Lunch, Dinner and Aperitifs, La Zanzara is open All Day Long and it’s “the” place for all you die-hard gourmand in near Vatican City.

http://www.lazanzararoma.com/en/
Pastasciutta

Steps away from St. Peter's, in the heart of Rione Borgo, Pastasciutta is a cozy restaurant where they look after each and every small detail, and their kitchen is completely open for you to watch as they produce the pasta from scratch and then prepare it, a captivating show that renews the whole Italian artisanal tradition.

http://www.pastasciuttaroma.it/en/index.html
Trattoria Vaticano Giggi

Giggi makes his Roman cuisine unique and economical, Giggi each day goes shopping addressing his trusted vendors: farmers or traders who choose quality as the main element and always have the best local produce.
